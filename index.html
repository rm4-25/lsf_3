--- layout: default title: "LSF Listening in/to South Florida" ---

How do our forms of connection to the environment reflect our relationship with it?

LSF is a digital environmental humanities project created by University of Miami doctoral students.The project connects the general public to South Florida through animal, urban, and elemental soundscapes embedded into an interactive map.
